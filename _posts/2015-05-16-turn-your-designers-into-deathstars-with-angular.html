The Conference

I wanted to share my talk Turn Your Designers Into Deathstars with Angular from ngVegas 2015. Making designers first class citizens in Angular is something I am particularly passionate about and thrilled that the Angular team is equally committed to this goal as well. I have created a series of exercises that I believe are appropriate for a designer to work through who is totally new to Angular. It is the most gentle approach to Angular I could think of and I would love to hear your thoughts!

Check out the talk and the slides and then please go through give the exercises a spin or share them with a designer you know who is looking to learn Angular.
